Core Governance Model

The "Overseer" Model

Agents perform 95% of the heavy lifting. Your single human overseer simply reviews exceptions and clicks "Approve."

The EMR Moat

PHI is fully protected by the Google BAA, but NextGen remains physically isolated to prevent destructive API overwrites.

Active Agents vs Passive RAG

We do not build chatbots. We build systems that analyze reality (e.g., employee census), make decisions, and execute workflows autonomously.
